The Eight fold group

Eight Fold Group is a leading civil and landscape contractor with over two decades of experience, operating across Maharashtra. Formerly known as Sushant Enterprises, the company specializes in a wide range of services, including civil construction, landscaping, interior design, and finishing work. With a team of professional engineers and consultants, Eight Fold Group delivers high-quality, cost-effective solutions, ensuring timely project completion. The company is committed to building trust through open communication and delivering 100% customer satisfaction across diverse sectors such as residential, industrial, and commercial projects.
